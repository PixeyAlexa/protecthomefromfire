Our Misson & Vision


Reduce carbon emissions

Hemp can help reduce carbon emissions by acting as a carbon sink, absorbing more CO2 than it releases during its growth. Additionally, hemp-based products, like biodegradable plastics and construction materials, offer eco-friendly alternatives to carbon-intensive materials.

Protect your home from fire

Hemp-based insulation materials can help protect your home from fire by being naturally fire-resistant. Additionally, using hempcrete for construction can provide a safer, eco-friendly alternative to traditional materials, reducing fire hazards.

Reduce your heating and air conditioning usage due to high R Values

Hemp insulation, with its high R-values, helps reduce heating and air conditioning usage by providing excellent thermal resistance. This leads to a more energy-efficient home, maintaining comfortable temperatures year-round with less energy consumption.
